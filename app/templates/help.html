Frequently Asked Questions

The system has three primary roles:

  • Admin: Has full access to the system, including user management, deleting records, and editing settings.
  • BHW (Barangay Health Worker): Can register residents, add consultations, and manage inventory.
  • Viewer (LGU Oversight): Can monitor all records and dashboard stats but cannot add, edit, or delete any data.

If your QR ID card isn't scanning, try the following:

  1. Ensure there is enough light in the room.
  2. Keep the ID card still for a few seconds.
  3. Clean your phone or computer's camera lens.

Backup Method: If the QR code still fails, click the "BHW Access" tab on the login page and enter your BHW Code and PIN manually.

To prevent accidental loss of important health data, only users with the Admin role are permitted to delete resident profiles or inventory items. If a mistake was made, please contact your supervisor or an Admin to correct it.
